About the Academy

Where Passion
Meets Precision

Founded in 2011, SONORIA has grown from a small studio in Brooklyn into one of the most respected music academies in the world. Our philosophy is simple: music is a language, and like any language, it requires immersion, practice, and the right mentors.

Every student receives a personalized curriculum designed around their goals — whether you dream of performing on stage, producing chart-topping hits, or simply playing for the joy of it.
